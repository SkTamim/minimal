Sand casting

Sand casting is one of the simplest types of casting with a long history. It is mainly used for producing large parts. It relies on silica-based materials. Such as naturally bonded sand, synthetic sand. It was been by filling a molten metal into the mold cavity that has been shaped from natural or synthetic sand. After cooling to get a workpiece.
